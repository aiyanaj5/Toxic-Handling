# Toxic-Handling
Toxic handling is a wearable glove that represents the relationship among the human body, technology, and the natural environment; when humidity comes into physical contact, the glove generates vibrations. Along with a separate system with ultrasonic sensors and LED lights. The vibrations and flashing LED lights represent the forever impactful loop between humans and the environment, each side causing harm/influence. Additionally, this project comments on the flooding in the Hawaiian islands during March of 2026 due to thousands of people having to directly interact with dangerous, flooded, and destroyed natural areas across Hawaii.
